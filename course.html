Our Core Academic Programs

Shree Sankalp provides specialized, high-intensity coaching for 11th & 12th Science and Diploma entrance, ensuring conceptual mastery and exam success in both Gujarati and English mediums.

11th & 12th Science (GSEB)

Our foundational program covers the entire GSEB syllabus for Physics, Chemistry, Math, and Biology, providing a rigorous base for future studies. Dedicated doubt-solving sessions included.

Gujarati & English Medium

We offer separate, parallel batches for both Gujarati and English mediums. Content, faculty, and focus remain identical, ensuring quality education regardless of language preference.

JEE / NEET / GUJCET Preparation

Integrated coaching for state and national-level engineering and medical entrance exams. Our curriculum is aligned to seamlessly prepare students for board exams and competitive tests.

Diploma Entrance

Targeted coaching for students aiming for direct entry into Diploma Engineering programs after 10th or 12th. Focused curriculum on core subjects required for entrance success.

Weekly Progress Monitoring

Consistent performance tracking through weekly tests, detailed report cards, and one-on-one sessions to identify weak areas and ensure steady academic growth.

Premium Study Materials

Access to our specially curated study modules, reference books, and exhaustive question banks designed by expert faculty to maximize learning efficiency.
